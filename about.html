02 : About the Series

Synopsis

This is the story of Indra, once whole, now navigating the debris of his own life. His parents dead, Indra takes revenge on the one responsible for their demise, and in the pursuit, he succumbs to the consequences, meeting his end. However, fate takes an unexpected turn as he reincarnates into a world pulsating with mana and magic. Yet, the enchanting surroundings can't dispel the shadows of his haunting past. In this poignant tale of resilience, Indra's journey becomes a testament to the indomitable spirit, forcing him to confront not only external challenges but the internal demons that threaten to consume him. Can he weave together the remnants of his life and find redemption in this new world that seeks to break him once more?

Story Theme

My series won't be about "good vs. evil" or "light vs. darkness." Instead, it will explore the gray areas of morality, where characters are often forced to choose between options that are all wrong. I want to create a realistic world where many times, no right choice exists. If Indra were faced with the decision to gain more power to protect those he loves or save a stranger, he would abandon the stranger without hesitation. There's no shame in inaction—only power matters, as Indra recalls:

Power cannot grant you happiness, but weakness cannot grant you anything.

Magic System

I plan to base my magic fantasy within the genre of Progression Fantasy, where our protagonist, Indra, grows stronger step by step throughout his journey. This genre emphasizes that the protagonist's strength and power play a key role in shaping the story. The magic system will feature clearly defined levels of power:

  1. Eternal (Strongest)
  2. Perpetual
  3. Ascendant
  4. Awakened
  5. Initiate
  6. Manaphyte (Weakest)

Those who don’t believe in magic will never find it.
