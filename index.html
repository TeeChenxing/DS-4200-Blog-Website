Hi there!

My name is Teera Tesharo. I am currently pursuing a Bachelors at Northeastern University and have been working on a multitude of projects concerning neural networks, web design and physics simulation. I also pursue other avenues of interest such as crafting pixel art. Though I am not the most talented at art, pixel art allows a beginner like me to get started and achieve relative success. I also have other hobbies such as flying. I am currently a pilot-in-training and one day wish to join the U.S. Air Force fighter pilot program to pursue my dream in aviation.

Beyond my love for art and aviation, I am also a bookworm. I have read over a hundred books since 2021 and my favorite genres are:

  • Fantasy
  • Romance
  • Comedy
  • Mystery
However, I must say that the genre of fantasy holds a special place in my heart as the first ever series I've read was the Throne of Glass series written by Sarah J. Maas which follows a female protagonist who fights to regain her freedom and liberate her homeland. This series draws inspiration from J.R.R Tolkien's Lord of the Rings but with a twist. Instead of the elves, we have the fae and instead of the orcs, we have the valg.

Currently, I am working on my own fantasy novel and hopefully will publish my first volume by 2026. I'll expand more on this in 02 : About the Series.
